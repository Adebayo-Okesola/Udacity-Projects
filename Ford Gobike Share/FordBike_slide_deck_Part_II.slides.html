Ford GoBike System Data¶

by Adebayo Okesola¶

Investigation Overview¶

In investigating the Ford GoBike Share dataset, a major point of concideration is to investigate the influence of Age of the riders on the distance covered and duration it takes to cover the distance. The main focus was on the distance (km), Age (years) and duration (hours).

Dataset Overview¶

The Ford GoBike System Data has 16 columns and 183411 rows and its latest update was February 2019 which is one of the data set provided by Udacity. The Ford GoBike data set includes information about individual rides made in a bike-sharing system covering the greater San Francisco Bay area. In this data set some rows were dropped at a certain point due to missing data which causes consistency and validity issue. Also two columns start and end time were dropped because theY will not be required for this analysis.

Relationship Between Distance and Duration¶

This is to show what relationship exist between distance covered by the riders and the time taken to cover this distance. It was observed that there is a weak positive correlation between distance and duration it takes the rider to cover the distance. Also more of the riders cover a distance of 8 km in less than 5 hours.
