Introduction¶

Dataset Description¶

This dataset contains the information from over 110k medical appointments in Brazil, and its associated with 14 variables. The most important of these variables if that which tells if the patient show-up or no show to the appointment. Therefore, it is neccessary to know why someone makes a doctor appointment, recieves all the instructions and no-show.

Below are the list of all columns name and what they signify in the dataset

PatientId: Identification of patient.

AppointmentID: Identification of eacch appointment.

Gender: Male or Female.

ScheduledDay: The day of actual appointment, where they have to see the doctor.

ApointmentDay: The day someone called or registered the appointment.

Age: How old is the patient.

Neighbourhood: Where the appointments takes place.

Scholarship: True or False

Hipertension: True or False

Diabetes: True or False

Alcoholism: True or False

Handcap: True or False

SMS_recieved: 1 or more messages sent to the patient.

No-show: Yes or No

Question(s) for Analysis¶

1.): What is the proportion and of the patients in dataset Showed up and Didn't show for their appointment?

2.): Which Age Category has the highest number of patients among the patient that showed up and those that didn't show up for the appointment

3.): What is the Age relationship between the patient that showed and those that didn't show up?

4.): What is the gender distribution of all patient in the dataset?

5.): Which ailment record has the highest number of patient Showed up and the lowest number of patients Show up?

6.): What age group has the Highest and lowest number of patient that showed and did not show?

7.): How does Date difference affect patient from Showing up for their appointment?

8.): What effect does SMS recieved by patients have on showing up or not showing up for thier appointment?

Data Cleaning¶

Having checked the general properties of the dataset, it was observed that some of the column's datatype such as "PatientId" which is a "float" needs to be changed to integers,"Appointment" and "ScheduleDay" needs be change to date time. Also some renaming of "No-show", "Handcap","AppointmentID","PatientId","Hipertension","ScheduledDay"and"AppointmentDay" column. Lastly, correcting of data input into the handicap column needs to be addressed since the "describe" function returns "Handcap" maximum value to be 4. Row with negative age and Scholarship column will dropped before any form of further analysis can be done on this dataset. This process is called data cleaning processes

Simplifying the No_show Column¶

The no show column seems a little confusing and it could leads to miss-interpretation of answer whether a patient shows up or not. From the dataset directory it was said to be either True or False but it was observed that in this column the "NO" value means the patient show-up while "YES" value means the patient didnt show up, this seems confusing. Therefore, for easy understanding of this variable there will be need to a amend the No_show column to answer direct Yes or No.
